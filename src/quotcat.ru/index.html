Our basics

Quotalogic is redefining the way we approach products. We believe that every physical product should have a single virtual counterpart, accessible to the entire dealer network, rather than millions of data copies for each seller.

Reimagining Proposals with Quotalogic

Quotalogic is revolutionizing the way we use proposals. They serve not just as vehicles for product information, but as the medium to share the products themselves.

Each of our Proposals is a custom-designed web page, equipped with specific tools tailored to the template and use case. They act as representative catalogs, promos, or price lists. Dealers can connect the offered products to their workspace with just one click and start using them. They can combine products from multiple suppliers, share them with their network, or create quotes for customers.

Moreover, Quotalogic Proposals can function as a company website, a public price list, or as a quick, customizable quote for a customer. They come with adaptive product descriptions and sales tools, such as chat, online payment, invoicing, and more.

Integrations In and Out

Quotalogic handles data synchronization and integrations. You can create a catalog that pulls data from any external source, whether it's via a feed, API, or a file. Simultaneously, you can export any data collected in Quotalogic to your website, CMS, or marketplace.

Dynamically Constructed
Product Data Model

Product characteristics can be determined by dependencies (using formulas) on other values or external factors. It's as easy as using a spreadsheet, but specifically tailored for sales tasks.

Evolutionary Omnichanelling

Distributors can customize and share different product properties with various dealer groups.

Dealers, in turn, can override and extend these characteristics before resharing them with partners.

This way, the core of the product remains consistent, while each seller in the chain sees a unique version of the product.

A Single Virtual Product for the Entire Dealer Network

With every dealer in the network using a single, shared foundational product object, any changes to product details are instantly reflected across the network.

Thanks to dynamic formulas that automatically calculate extended product characteristics, your product information always remains up-to-date, requiring no manual management.

Secured User Data

If required, sellers can alter or disconnect the dependency of product data on the supplier. Any updates or changes will continue to be forwarded to the seller, who can easily choose to accept, reject or merge them with their current data.

Smart quoting

While compiling a quote, the system recommends specific products and pricing, drawing on supplier guidance, machine learning, and an analysis of similar product combinations used within the dealer network.

With an innovative approach to data management, we create a comprehensive workspace for both B2B and B2C sales, equipped with PIM, quoting, and price management tools, tailored specifically to the needs of distributors and dealers.
